Can You Perform Outside?

Outside is no problem at all, we've done it many times in the past.

However, there must also be some form of shelter for the electrical equipment in order to setup outside, this is in case of rain or a sudden change in weather. Our insurance won't let us proceed without it.

We do also need power for the sound equipment - (We can provide extension cables if needed)

Without either of that the only thing we would be able to offer would be our 'Mix and Mingle Balloon Modelling' package which can be performed anywhere regardless of weather, shelter or power.

I hope this helps!
